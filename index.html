Waste Awareness

Know Your Waste

Proper segregation is the first step towards a cleaner planet.

💧

Wet Waste

Biodegradable waste that decomposes naturally.

  • 🥦 Fruit & vegetable peels
  • 🍳 Cooked food leftovers
  • 🌿 Garden / plant waste
  • ☕ Tea bags, coffee grounds
  • 🥚 Eggshells
💡 Use green bins for wet waste.
📦

Dry Waste

Non-biodegradable waste that can often be recycled.

  • 📰 Paper, cardboard, newspapers
  • 🍾 Plastic bottles & containers
  • 🥫 Metal cans & tins
  • 🪟 Glass bottles & jars
  • 👗 Cloth & fabric scraps
💡 Use blue bins for dry waste.
☣️

Hazardous Waste

Waste requiring special disposal. Never mix with regular bins.

  • 🔋 Batteries & electronics
  • 💊 Expired medicines
  • 🎨 Paints, chemicals
  • 💡 Bulbs & tube lights
  • 🧴 Aerosol cans
💡 Use red bins for hazardous waste.
